ENC 2135 Writing Portfolio

Danny Ward | Spring 2026

This is my writing portfolio from ENC 2135. It has my investigative essay on U.S. stealth military doctrine, a multigenre campaign I built around that research, some supporting documents from the process, and a reflection on what I learned. The stealth topic ended up being a lot more interesting than I expected when I picked it. What started as a history of cool military technology turned into something I actually wanted to argue about, which made the whole semester go a lot better than it might have otherwise.

Project 1

Investigative essay on the evolution of U.S. stealth doctrine and the case for Mosaic Warfare

Project 2

A multigenre persuasive campaign adapting the research for military and defense audiences

Artifacts

Supporting documents showing research and planning process

Reflection

A 1000+ word statement on what I learned about genre, composing, and rhetoric
